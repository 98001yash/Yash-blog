## Speed is a systems outcome

Teams rarely slow down because they lack raw execution ability. More often, speed erodes when every new page, feature, or content type requires bespoke handling.

## Build the stable pieces first

A good portfolio platform mirrors the same principle used in product codebases:

1. Establish layout primitives.
2. Centralize content loading.
3. Create reusable cards and detail templates.
4. Add metadata generation close to the route layer.

## The compounding effect

When those pieces are stable, content creation becomes operationally simple. Adding a new article or case study is just a new `.mdx` file plus frontmatter.
